About me

The mind behind
FreshMint

Video Editor · Strategist · Gamer · Kanpur, India

18 years old. Self-taught. Driven by a simple belief — good work builds real connections, and real connections build everything else. FreshMint isn't just a service. It's the project I've been quietly building towards my whole life.

Origin story

It started with MS Paint. Age 4 or 5, on my family's business laptop — before most kids were even using a computer, I was already figuring out how pixels worked. That curiosity never left.

Video editing came through school annual competitions — that pressure of making something look good on a deadline, in front of an audience. It clicked instantly. Gaming was never just entertainment either — it was always about systems, strategy, and understanding how things work.

Business wasn't something I learned from a book. It was a mindset that came from observing the world — how people trade value, build trust, and grow. FreshMint is where all three of those things finally meet.
