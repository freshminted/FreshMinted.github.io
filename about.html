What I do

Video editing — from short-form social content to long-form YouTube and cinematic cuts. Adobe Premiere Pro, DaVinci Resolve, After Effects. Every edit is colour graded, music-synced and export-ready for any platform.

Business strategy — social media audits, content calendars, competitor analysis and growth plans delivered as a clear, actionable document. No vague advice — specific steps, specific platforms, specific timelines.

Web development — pure HTML, CSS and JavaScript. No WordPress, no monthly fees, no framework bloat. Fast, responsive, maintainable code. FreshMint itself scores 100/100 on desktop PageSpeed.

Game strategy — specialist coaching for Cities: Skylines, Rise of Industries, Ranch Simulator and other simulation and strategy games. VOD reviews, layout guides and bug workarounds.
